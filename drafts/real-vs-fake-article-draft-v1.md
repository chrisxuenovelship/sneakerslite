Why? APAC has:

## The Current Authentication Problem: Why It Fails

Platforms like **StockX, GOAT, Novelship, and SNKRDUNK** all use authentication. But the system has three critical flaws:

### 1. **Human Authenticators: Inconsistent & Slow**

- **How it works:** Trained experts examine physical shoes, checking materials, stitching, labeling, etc.
- **The problem:**
  - Counterfeiters improve faster than human training updates
  - High variance in expert judgment (one expert passes, another rejects the same shoe)
  - Expensive (~$8-15 per item), so platforms understaff and create bottlenecks
  - Takes 3-5 days, slowing the resale cycle

**Result:** ~5-8% of authentication disputes end in false positives (legitimate shoes rejected) or false negatives (fakes passing through).

### 2. **Hardware Tools: Incomplete Detection**

Some platforms use UV lights, magnification, spectral analysis, etc.

- **The problem:**
  - Counterfeiters copy materials that fool basic tools
  - Most tools can't detect modern fake materials that respond similarly to light/UV as real materials
  - No single hardware tool catches all fake types

**Result:** Hardware alone catches only 40-60% of sophisticated counterfeits.

### 3. **Digital Photos + Human Decision: Slowest & Most Expensive**

Sellers submit photos, humans review. This takes 5-10 days.

- **The problem:**
  - Photos can be deceiving or AI-generated
  - Lighting and angles hide flaws
  - High labor cost = platforms can't scale
  - Resellers hate the wait; conversion drops when listings sit in "pending" for a week

**Result:** Slowest option, high cost, still error-prone.
