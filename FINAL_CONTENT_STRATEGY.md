## Why Two Articles?

### The Buyer Journey Problem
**If we only had V1:**
- Captures casual buyers, enthusiasts, small-time resellers
- Misses platform decision-makers (CTOs, CFOs, product teams)
- No ROI data, no API discussion, no scale narrative
- Result: Lost partnership + API revenue

**If we only had V2:**
- Captures platforms, partnerships, enterprise buyers
- Misses individual sellers who want to verify their pairs
- No practical authentication guidance
- Result: Lost transactional revenue + brand reach

**With V1 + V2:**
- V1 captures 90% of search traffic (high volume, lower intent)
- V2 captures 10% of search traffic (lower volume, higher intent, higher value)
- Cross-linking between articles multiplies SEO power
- Different CTAs for different audiences = better conversion
