## Key Insights Delivered

### 1. Tiered Counterfeit Economics
- **Budget fakes** ($20–$80): Fail obvious checks; 60% of counterfeits
- **Mid-tier fakes** ($80–$200): Pass basic visual; fail advanced checks; 30% of counterfeits
- **High-tier fakes** ($200–$400+): Pass 70%+ of visual checks; only fail lab testing; 10% of counterfeits

### 2. Authentication Accuracy Ceiling
- **Visual checks alone:** 75–80% accuracy max
- **Professional lab testing:** 99.96% accuracy (SNKRDUNK)
- **Confidence scoring:** We'll tell you our confidence level (92% vs 99%), not just binary verdict

### 3. Check Reliability Over Time
- **R & D connection:** 88% accurate in 2018; <20% accurate in 2026 (fixed by factories)
- **Hourglass shape:** 72% accurate; easily faked at high tier
- **Perforations:** 85% accurate; manufacturing quality is harder to fake

### 4. Novelship Differentiation
- Combine visual + lab + comparative analysis + material verification
- Provide confidence scoring (not just "authentic/fake")
- Position as "honest authenticator" (admits limitations vs. overselling visual checks)
